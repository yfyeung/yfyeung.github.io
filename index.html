Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), a member of Cross Media (X-)Language Intelligence Lab (X-LANCE) in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, under the supervision of Prof. Xie Chen, and the leadership of Prof. Kai Yu.

Previously, I worked at Xiaomi AI lab as an algorithm engineer intern during my senior undergraduate year, developing the Next-gen Kaldi under the leadership of Dr. Daniel Povey.

My research is supported by CIE-Tencent Doctoral Research Incentive Project, and my current research interests include the following topics. I'm always open to collaboration and discussion. Please feel free to reach out.

  • Speech/Text Large Language Models

  • Speech Representation Learning / Speech Tokenization (Continuous and Discrete)

  • Language Modeling for Text-to-Speech Synthesis

  • Multilingual Speech Recognition
